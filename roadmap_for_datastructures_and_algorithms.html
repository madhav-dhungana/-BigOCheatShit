Roadmap To Learn DSA From Scratch

Data structures and algorithms are branches of computer science that focus on writing efficient and optimized computer programs. Data structure refers to the organization and storage of data, while algorithms deal with the procedures to achieve desired results or outcomes.

The ultimate goal of a software engineer is to write efficient code. Therefore, understanding data structures and algorithms will only make someone a software engineer. In other words, expertise or deep knowledge of data structures and algorithms makes you a software engineer.

If your goal is to innovate or build new things, or to become a computer scientist, achieving that goal is impossible without knowledge of DSA."
